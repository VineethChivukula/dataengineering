# Data Vs Information

You see, before we know what Data Engineering is, we have to understand what Data means and how is it different from Information.

## Data

It is defined as raw, unprocessed facts and figures that have no meaning.

For example, if I just give you a series of numbers say 168, 170, 173, 177 won't you get a question in your mind like *"what are they?"*, and *"what do they represent?"*. These numbers alone tell you nothing.

## Information

It is defined as the data which has context and meaning. It answers the questions that you had above.

Considering previous example, if I attach *cm* to each of them, it will make sense i.e., 168 cm, 170 cm, 173 cm, 177 cm. This information is structured and serves some purpose.
